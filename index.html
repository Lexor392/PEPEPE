PEPEPE is a P2E (play-to-earn) game where players can control a 3D PEPE character and engage in various activities to earn cryptocurrency. The game is a virtual world where players can communicate with each other, participate in activities, buy and sell items with in-game currency.

PEPE is the main character of the game, which can be along with other players. He can participate in various quests, games and battles to earn cryptocurrency. In addition, players can create their own missions and quests for other players to participate in and earn rewards.
